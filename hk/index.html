THE HACKTRAIN IS COMING TO HONG KONG!


GET READY FOR THE BIGGEST HACKATHON OF THE YEAR!


HACKTRAIN:
/'HAK'TREɪN/
Noun.



The HackTrain 3.0 is arguably one of the craziest and most memorable hackathon experiences on earth! A weekend-long adventure hacking whilst on moving trains! 120 of the very best developers, designers and entrepreneurs will be building apps, websites and hardware that tackles the industry's biggest challenges!


48 HOURS. 8 VENUES. 40 PARTICIPANTS. #HACKTRAINHK



The HackTrain is the world's best transport hackathon. Participants take part in an immersive experience travelling from venue to venue whilst building new technology solving some of the biggest challenges that the transport industry faces.

The initiative is designed to improve customer experience and operational efficiency for passengers and transport companies.

Journeys


THE HACKTRAIN HK ROUTE WILL BE ANNOUNCED SOON!


The HackTrain will start in the heart of Hong Kong. The participants will then travel around Hong Kong via train and other modes of transport. Last year's participants in the HackTrain UK started at the St. Pancras Renaissance Hotel in London, and finished at the National Railway Museum in York.

Challenges


LEARN MORE ABOUT THE HACKATHON CHALLENGES! THEY WILL BE ANNOUNCED SOON!


The hackathons will provide challenges and datasets to the participants of the hackathon. This will give the participants an opportunity to work on things that can actually have an impact in the whole industry!

Prizes


Last year's prizes for the HackTrain UK included a fully paid trip to Singapore to the Tech in Asia conference, trips to Amsterdam to speak at internationall conferences, and lightsabers, hoverboards and pebble watches!
