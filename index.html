Our Services

Hackathon Events

Collectively we've run over 50 hackathons. The HackTrain Hackathon, is an annual 48-hour immersive event that takes place on a moving train that attracts hundreds of top software and hardware engineers, designers and entrepreneurs. Teams on the hackathon tackle some of the major challenges in the rail industry.

Learn More

Digital Transformation Projects

Working as a technology consultancy, we provide services across the rail industry and deliver large-scale technology projects after successfully completing trials and feasibility studies. Working with a number of tran operators, our projects have improved station enhancement, customer experience and operational efficiency.

Learn More

Industry Reports

We research and construct reports on the rail industry. The B.A.R.R.I.E.R.S. Report is the most recent report released by the HackTrain. It studies the barriers to innovation in the rail industry and provides actionable recommendations to combat these barriers and revitalise entrepreneuriship and innovation.
